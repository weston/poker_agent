===Hand Stats===
All-in:
	flg_allin
	A check indicates that the player was all-in at some point in the hand.
	
All-in Equity:
	val_allin_equity * 100.0
	Equity in the pot that a player had at the time the all-in occurred in the hand.  This value is blank if an all-in did not occur in the hand.
	
All-in Equity Adjusted BB:
	amt_expected_bb_won
	Expected amount of money that a player would have won from this hand in big blinds if, at the time of the all-in, players were returned their equity in the pot instead of running out any remaining cards. Note: This value will be the same as BB Won for all hands where there was either no all-in, or the all-in occurred on the river.
	
BB Won
amt_bb_won
Number of big blinds won in the given hand by the player.

BB Won (Including Payouts)
amt_bb_won_inc_payout
Number of big blinds won in the given hand by the player.  Payouts are included in the amount won calculations.

Big Blind
flg_blind_b
A check indicates that the player in the hand was in the big blind.

Board
lookup_cards(id_flop1, id_flop2, id_flop3, id_turn, id_river)
Displays any or all of the community cards from the hand.

Chips in BBs
amt_chips / amt_bb
Number of chips the player started the hand with in terms of big blinds.

Cold Call 2Bet PF
flg_p_ccall
A check indicates that the player cold called a 2Bet preflop.  An open box indicates that the player could have cold called a 2Bet preflop but elected not to.

Date
date_played
Date the hand was played (calculated using the player statistics database table).

Date Summary
date_played_summary
Date the hand was played (calculated using the hand summary database table).

Dead Blind
flg_blind_db or flg_blind_ds
A check indicates that the player posted a dead large or small blind during the hand.

Final Hand
lookup_handrankval(id_final_hand, flg_showed, enum_folded)
Displays the strength of the final hand that a player held when the hand went to showdown, or why the hand was not shown.

Flop Action
str_actions_f
Displays the action that the player took in the hand on the flop.  Each action is represented by a letter: X = Check, B = Bet, C = Call, F = Fold, R = Raise.

Flop Call
flg_f_call
A check indicates that the player called a bet or raise on the flop.  An open box indicates that the player could have called a bet or raise on the flop but elected not to.

Flop Cards
lookup_cards_sort(id_flop1, id_flop2, id_flop3)
Displays the three flop cards from the hand if they were dealt graphically according to your theme.

Flop Check
flg_f_check
A check indicates that the player checked on the flop.  An open box indicates that the player had a chance to check on the flop but elected not to.

Flop Check Raise
flg_check_raise_f
A check indicates that the player checked and then raised on the flop.  An open box indicates that the player checked and had a chance to raise on the flop but elected not to.

Flop Donk
flg_f_donk
A check indicates that the player donk bet on the flop.  An open box indicates that the player could have donk bet on the flop but elected not to.

Flop Float
flg_f_float
A check indicates that the player float bet on the flop.  An open box indicates that the player could have float bet on the flop but elected not to.

Flop Fold
flg_f_fold
A check indicates that the player folded on the flop.  An open box indicates that the player had a chance to fold on the flop.

Flop Raise
flg_f_raise
A check indicates that the player raised on the flop.  An open box indicates that the player had a chance to raise on the flop.

Flop SPR
amt_spr_f
When the player saw the flop, the ratio of the player's starting stack size at the start of the flop to the size of the pot at that time.

Game Currency All-In Equity Adjusted
amt_expected_won
Expected amount of money that a player would have won from this hand in the currency the hand was played if, at the time of the all-in, players were returned their equity in the pot instead of running out any remaining cards. Note: This value will be the same as Amount Won for all hands where there was either no all-in, or the all-in occurred on the river.

Game Currency Amount in Stack
amt_chips
Total amount of money the player started the hand with in the originaly currency the hand was played.

Game Currency Bet Flop
amt_bet_f
Total amount of money bet by the player on the flop in the hand in the original currency the hand was played.

Game Currency Bet Preflop
amt_bet_p
Amount of money bet by the player preflop in the hand in the original currency the hand was played.

Game Currency Bet River
amt_bet_r
Total amount of money bet by the player on the river in the hand in the original currency the hand was played.

Game Currency Bet Turn
amt_bet_t
Total amount of money bet by the player on the turn in the hand in the original currency the hand was played.

Game Currency Jackpot
amt_jackpot
Total amount of jackpot fee that was taken out of the hand in the currency the hand was originally played.

Game Currency Payout
amt_payout
Total amount of payout received in the hand in the currency the hand was originally played.

Game Currency Payout Fee
amt_payout_fee
Total amount of payout fees paid in the hand in the currency the hand was originally played.

Game Currency Player Rake
amt_rake_player
Total amount of rake that was paid by the player who won the hand in the currency the hand was originally played.

Game Currency Pot Size
amt_pot
Total amount of money that was in the pot in hand in the currency the hand was originally played.

Game Currency Rake
amt_rake
Total amount of rake that was taken out of the hand in the currency the hand was originally played.

Game Currency Rake Contributed
amt_rake_weighted_contributed
Total amount of money removed from the pot as rake divided porportionately by the amount of money the player contributed to the pot.

Game Currency Rake Share
amt_rake_share
Total amount of money removed from the pot as rake divided by the number of players dealt into the hand calculated in the currency the hand was originally played.

Game Currency Straddle
amt_straddle
Total amount of straddle placed in the hand in the currency the hand was originally played.

Game Currency Won
amt_won
Amount of money won in the given hand in the original currency the hand was played.

Game Currency Won (Including Payouts)
amt_won_inc_payout
Amount of money won in the given hand in the original currency the hand was played.  Payouts are included in the amount won calculations.

Game Type
id_gametype
Game that was played - Holdem or Omaha (calculated using the player statistics database table).

Game Type Summary
id_gametype_summary
Game that was played - Holdem or Omaha (calculated using the hand summary database table).

Hand
id_holecard
Player's starting hand displayed in text notation (for example: 43o, J8s, etc.)

hand_no
Number of the hand that was played.
